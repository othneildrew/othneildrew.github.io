About Othneil

Othneil Drew is an extremely creative individual with a strong background in UI/UX design, product lifecycle management and strategic planning/marketing.

Yes, I'm going to speak about myself in the third person :)

He has successfully lead high-performing teams and improve frontend workflow as a team lead — allowing the team to release key updates to customers more frequently.

He is the founder of Student Pilot Portal, an online platform that enables aspiring aviators to learn about aviation, share their passion for flight, and connect with others worldwide.

With 900+ hours of volunteered service to the aviation community, he serves as an advocate for promoting youths education in the industry and is actively involved with many organizations like the Organization of Black Aerospace Professionals, Virgin Islands Chapter Tuskegee Airmen, Inc., U.S. Dream Academy, and the United States Coast Guard Auxiliary.

Drew currently works as a Software Engineer in Houston Texas. His other interests are flight simulation, photography, gaming, guitar, and the show NCIS: Los Angeles.
